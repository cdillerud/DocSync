THE GUARDRAILS (please read these before the session)
-----------------------------------------------------

These are the rules that keep the pilot safe.

  - Do NOT click "Post to BC." Posting writes real entries to
    Business Central; it is not part of this pilot.
  - Do NOT click "Mark Ready" or "Save" unless IT specifically
    asks you to on a single document.
  - Test only the documents on the assigned list we hand you.
  - Report every observation through the feedback CSV — even
    small things. One row per document.
  - If a real payment is at risk because of something you see
    in the Hub, call your AP supervisor first, then fall back to
    Square9 and process the payment there. Hub bugs do not
    block real payments.

These rules will also be on the kickoff handout we hand you in
the room.

WHAT TO EXPECT IN THE HUB
-------------------------

When you open a document, you will see:

  - The filename at the top.
  - A "Document Status" card.
  - An "AP Review" panel with five fields: Vendor, Invoice #,
    Invoice Date, Total Amount, PO Number.
  - The PDF preview.

Your job is to compare each field in the AP Review panel to the
PDF and tell us whether it matches. That's it.
